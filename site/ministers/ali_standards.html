About This Metric

In 2024-25, 52% of high-volume Government of Canada services (45,000+ transactions/year) met their published service standards (target: 80%), down from 55% in 2023-24. Reporting shifted from priority to high-volume services under the Policy on Service and Digital. Across all services, individual service standards were met 73% of the time.
